Zijian Wu

                        

I am a second year Robotics M.S.E. student at Johns Hopkins University. Before that, I was a research assistant at the School of Automation Engineering, the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), for one year. I obtained my B.E. degree in Mechatronics Engineering at UESTC, in 2020. I'm interested in applying robotics in the field of medicine and surgery combined with computer vision, machine learning, and immersive technologies. My research goal is to create a computer-integrated surgical (CIS) system enabling robots to perceive special surgical or medical scenario and to decide rational actions for clinical tasks.
